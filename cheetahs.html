Illegal Cheetah Trade (2010-2019)

This visualisation presents data from ten years (2010-2019) of illegal cheetah trade. The dataset records trades of live cheetahs and cheetah body parts, such as skin and skulls. The global number of cheetahs in the wild has reduced drastically over the last century. As an endagered species, trade of wild cheetahs and cheetah parts is banned. However, covert illegal trades still occur.
